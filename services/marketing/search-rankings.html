The Two Tracks

One program. Two search surfaces.

Traditional SEO still compounds. AEO is the new frontier. We run them together because the signals overlap — great content wins both, but each has its own playbook.

Track 01 — SEO

Rank in Search

The blue-link game isn't dead — it's just more competitive. We win it with the fundamentals done at a professional level.

  • Technical SEO — site speed, Core Web Vitals, crawlability, indexation, canonicals, redirects, schema.
  • Keyword strategy — commercial intent mapping, cluster architecture, topical authority over scattered posts.
  • Content engine — pillar pages, supporting content, internal linking, on-page optimization tied to business outcomes.
  • Authority + backlinks — earned links from real publications, digital PR, partnerships that compound over years.
  • Local + map pack — GBP optimization, review velocity, location pages, citations for multi-location businesses.

Track 02 — AEO

Rank in AI

AI engines don't rank — they answer. Getting cited means writing content models can actually quote, inside a surface they actually trust.

  • Citability engineering — content structured for LLM retrieval: clear definitions, direct answers, named entities, quotable blocks.
  • Structured data + llms.txt — JSON-LD schema, FAQ/HowTo/Organization markup, and an llms.txt file telling AI engines what to use.
  • Knowledge-graph alignment — Wikipedia, Wikidata, G2, Crunchbase, review sites — where AI models verify you actually exist and what you do.
  • Prompt-surface tracking — monitor what ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini actually say about your category and brand. Adjust the source material.
  • Original research + POV — AI engines reward sources that say something unique. We help produce content with a defensible point of view, not paraphrased filler.
