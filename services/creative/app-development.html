What It Is

Custom tools. Built for leverage.

App development at Revelation is about building the piece of software that doesn't exist yet but should — the internal tool, the customer portal, the quoting engine, the dashboard — that removes a bottleneck or creates an advantage competitors don't have.

We also build and connect business systems end-to-end: CRMs, automation workflows, AI integrations, lead-handling pipelines. Not decoration. Machinery.

"Software that earns its place."

Built for the Actual Job

No generic SaaS shoehorned into a business that doesn't fit it. Tools scoped to the real workflow, the real user, and the real data you already have.

Connected to Everything Else

Integrations with your CRM, billing, comms, and data layer — so the new tool isn't a silo; it's part of the machine.

AI & Automation-Aware

Built to leverage the current generation of automation and AI — not as a gimmick, but where it genuinely removes friction, speeds decisions, or reduces manual work.

Ownable and Maintainable

You own the build. Clean code, documentation, and handoff — so the business isn't locked into Revelation to keep the lights on a year from now.

What We Deliver

Six types of builds.

From customer-facing products to internal operational tools, mobile apps, and team training systems — we build the software that fits the operation, starting from a working MVP and scaling into a real platform if it earns its way there.

01 / Deliverable

Customer-Facing Web Apps

Portals, dashboards, booking tools, quoting engines, membership platforms — apps your customers use, designed to strengthen the relationship and speed up the buying cycle.

02 / Deliverable

Internal Tools & Portals

Operations dashboards, admin panels, pricing calculators, job trackers — the internal software that turns a messy spreadsheet workflow into a tool the team actually wants to use.

03 / Deliverable

Mobile Apps — iOS, Android, PWA

Native iOS and Android apps, plus progressive web apps built mobile-first. Field tools, customer apps, driver apps, dispatcher apps — built for the truck, the warehouse, the jobsite, not just the desk.

04 / Deliverable

Custom Integrations

API integrations, data pipelines, and connectors that make your tools talk to each other — CRM ↔ billing, website ↔ pipeline, ads ↔ reporting.

05 / Deliverable

Training Systems & Learning Portals

Internal training platforms, onboarding portals, SOP libraries, certification tracking, and team learning systems — so new hires ramp fast and institutional knowledge doesn't walk out the door with turnover.

06 / Deliverable

MVP Scoping & Build

From idea to working product — we scope, design, build, and launch an MVP that tests the real hypothesis without spending a year and six figures on a platform nobody asked for.

Business Systems & Automation

One platform. Your entire business system.

GoHighLevel is a platform. Revelation turns it into a system. For operators not on GHL, we build the equivalent on whatever CRM fits the operation — HubSpot, Close, Pipedrive, Salesforce, custom. Same principle either way: stop stitching together seven tools; run the business through one connected system.

GHL Deep Setup

CRM, automations, pipelines, calendars, forms, funnels — configured and connected as one operating system, not a pile of disconnected features.

CRM Workflow Architecture

For any CRM — not just GHL. Pipeline structure, stage logic, deal flow, and team routing designed around how sales actually happens in your business.

Pipeline & Lead Routing

Every new lead lands in the right pipeline, with the right stage, in front of the right person — automatically. No more leads dying in someone's inbox.

Follow-Up Automation Sequences

Email, SMS, voicemail drop, task triggers — multi-channel follow-up that runs whether the sales team remembers or not.

AI & Automation Integration

AI-assisted lead qualification, intelligent routing, automated responses, and workflow triggers that reduce manual touches without losing the human judgment where it matters.

Lead Handling End-to-End

From first touch to close to post-sale follow-up — a connected system so nothing falls through the cracks and every lead is accounted for.

GHL is the platform. Revelation is the build team that turns it into a real operating system for your business — and builds the equivalent on any CRM if GHL isn't the right fit.
Who It's For

For operators whose tools are holding them back.

App & systems engagements are best for businesses who've outgrown spreadsheets, duct-taped SaaS stacks, or generic CRMs — and who are ready to have the tool match the operation instead of the other way around.

  • Operations teams running the business through spreadsheets, email threads, and memory
  • Companies paying for six SaaS tools that don't talk to each other
  • Founders with a product idea that needs an MVP built, not another pitch deck
  • Businesses on GHL or any CRM that want it to actually run the operation, not just hold contacts
  • Service businesses losing leads between the website form and the first follow-up call
