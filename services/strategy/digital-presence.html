Strategy · Sprint 03

You can't convert an audience that can't find you.

Digital presence is the map — where you show up, what shows up when someone searches your name, how your funnel surfaces reinforce each other, and whether any of it is actually compounding. Most businesses have a website and scattered profiles. That's not presence. Presence is engineered visibility across every surface a buyer might land on.

Book a Strategy Session See Deliverables
What It Is

Surface area, engineered.

Your buyer doesn't decide based on one touchpoint. They Google you, check LinkedIn, skim reviews, lurk your site, find a podcast clip, notice your content, maybe read a teardown someone else wrote about you. Every one of those surfaces is either reinforcing the decision or fighting it.

Digital presence is the discipline of auditing every surface, deciding which ones matter, and engineering the ecosystem so they all carry the same story, the same credibility, and the same path forward. No random profiles, no orphan pages, no dead funnels.

"Every buyer does a background check. Control what they find."

Discoverability

When someone searches your name, your category, or your problem space — you show up, on the right surfaces, with the right first impression.

Authority Signals

Reviews, press, portfolio, case work, LinkedIn depth — the signals that make buyers trust you before they ever talk to you.

Ecosystem Coherence

Your site, your socials, your sales assets — all carrying the same message instead of three different versions of the business.

Funnel Integrity

Every surface has a clear next step — and the next step actually works. No 404s, no dead CTAs, no "call us" as the only conversion.

What We Deliver

Four documented systems.

A digital presence engagement produces a complete map of your current surface area and a prioritized plan for the surfaces that will actually move revenue.

01 / System

Digital Presence Audit

A full inventory of every surface your brand lives on — site, socials, directories, reviews, press, search results — with a honest read on what's working, what's leaking, and what's invisible.

02 / System

Website Ecosystem Strategy

Your site as a system, not a brochure. Information architecture, conversion surfaces, content hierarchy, and how the whole thing plugs into the rest of your marketing.

03 / System

Online Authority Plan

The specific authority signals you need to build — reviews, case studies, LinkedIn presence, press, speaking — sequenced so each one compounds the next.

04 / System

Funnel Surface Mapping

Every path from awareness to action — mapped end to end, with gaps, dead ends, and broken handoffs identified and prioritized for fixes.

Who It's For

Built for businesses tired of being invisible.

Digital presence engagements are best for operators whose buyers are already researching them — and who know the first impression isn't what they want it to be.

  • Companies where the website is a brochure, not a conversion engine
  • Founders who know buyers are Googling them but haven't looked at what shows up in a year
  • Businesses with scattered, half-maintained presence across 6+ platforms
  • Teams about to launch something big and realizing the surface area isn't ready
  • Operators who generate leads from referrals but nothing from digital gravity
