**Information you provide to us.** When you contact us, request a quote, or become a client, we may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, mailing address, billing details, and the contents of messages you send us.

**Information collected automatically.** When you visit our website, we and our analytics and advertising providers may automatically collect device and usage information such as IP address, browser type, pages viewed, referring URLs, and similar data through cookies and comparable technologies.

**Information from third-party advertising platforms.** When a client grants us authorized access to their advertising accounts (for example, a Meta Business account, ad account, or Facebook Page), we access and process data made available through those platforms in order to provide our services. This may include advertising campaign settings and performance metrics (such as spend, impressions, clicks, and cost per result), audience and targeting configuration, and lead information submitted by users through lead forms or instant forms associated with the client's campaigns. We access this data only as permitted by the client and in accordance with the platform's terms.

## How we use information

We use the information described above to:

- Provide, operate, and improve our marketing, advertising-management, and automation services;
- Create, publish, optimize, and report on advertising campaigns on behalf of our clients;
- Retrieve leads generated by client campaigns and route them into the client's customer relationship management (CRM) system in a timely manner;
- Communicate with you, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support;
- Process payments and maintain business records;
- Analyze website usage and improve our website and offerings;
- Detect, prevent, and address security issues, fraud, or violations of our terms; and
- Comply with legal obligations.

## Platform data and our obligations

When we access data through the Meta Platform, the Google advertising platforms, or any similar service on behalf of a client, we handle that data in accordance with the applicable platform's terms and policies, including the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We use platform data solely to provide the agreed services to the client who authorized the access. We do not sell platform data, and we do not use it for any purpose unrelated to the services we provide to that client.

## How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

- **With service providers** who perform services on our behalf (such as hosting, analytics, CRM, payment processing, and communications), under obligations to protect the information;
- **With advertising and technology platforms** (such as Meta and Google) as necessary to deliver and manage campaigns we run for clients;
- **With our clients**, where the information relates to services performed for that client (for example, leads generated by their campaigns);
- **For legal reasons**, when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Revelation Agency, our clients, or others; and
- **In a business transfer**, in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

## Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide our services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we delete or de-identify it. Platform data accessed on behalf of a client is retained only as long as necessary to provide the services to that client, or as required by the platform's terms.

## Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us at **connect@revelationagency.com**. We will respond consistent with applicable law. You may also opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in any such email.

**For data we process on behalf of a client:** if you are an individual whose information was collected through one of our clients' campaigns (for example, you submitted a lead form), your information is controlled by that client. We will refer your request to the relevant client or assist them in responding.

## Children's privacy

## International users
