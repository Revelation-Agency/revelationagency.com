01 — Context

A Website Built to Win Local Solar Search

  • ClientNet Metering Systems
  • IndustrySolar & Renewable Energy
  • Year2026
  • Live SiteNetMeteringSystems.com

Net Metering Systems needed a website that could stand up in one of the most competitive local service verticals — residential solar installation. In Clovis, homeowners searching for solar installation have no shortage of options, and the difference between winning and losing a lead often comes down to first impression and page authority.

We built NetMeteringSystems.com from scratch — fully custom-coded for performance, mobile-first, and engineered to rank. Every element of the site was built to convert residential visitors into estimate requests: clear service areas, direct calls to action, project proof, and fast load times.

The site serves as the anchor for the full digital system — receiving traffic from paid ads, organic search, and social, then converting visitors into leads that flow directly into the GHL automation pipeline.

02 — Services Delivered

What we shipped.

Custom Coded Frontend

Fully hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no templates, no page builders. Built for performance and full design control.

Mobile-First Responsive Design

Designed and tested across all device sizes — the majority of solar leads come from mobile, and the site is built for it.

Local SEO Architecture

Structured for Clovis-area solar search — service area pages, schema markup, and technical SEO foundations to rank and hold position.

Lead Conversion Optimization

Clear calls to action, trust signals, project proof, and direct quote request flows — built to move visitors from curious to contacted.
