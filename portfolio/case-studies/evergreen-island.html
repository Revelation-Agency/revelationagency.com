Evergreen Island
Event Venue Acquisition

Event Venue · 2 Years · $10/lead
01 — Context

Evergreen Island

  • ClientEvergreen Island
  • IndustryEvent Venue / Hospitality
  • LocationCentral Valley, CA
  • Year2024

Evergreen Island is an event venue that needed reliable inbound during the wedding-booking season and again for corporate calendars in the off-months.

Revelation Agency ran $10/lead Facebook ads for two years, produced a venue tour video that anchors every paid placement, and built a GoHighLevel booking automation that checks venue availability and confirms appointments without staff involvement.

After a management change, the venue came back to rebuild parts of the system — proof of the engagement’s strength.
